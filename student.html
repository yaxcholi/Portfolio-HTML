Student Services

Welcome to the Student Services page! Here, you can find all the essential resources and forms to support your journey at Greenfield University.

Important Forms

  • Prior Approval Form - Request approval for your course or visit.
  • Leave Request Form - Apply for personal or medical leave.
  • Course Change Form - Request a change in your course schedule.

Financial Aid and Scholarships

If you need financial support or are interested in applying for scholarships, visit the Financial Aid page for more information.

Advising

Our advisors are here to assist you with academic planning. Schedule an appointment with an advisor through the Advising Services page.

Student Health and Wellness

Your health is important to us! For more information about health services available to students, please visit the Health and Wellness page.
